Why is it important to keep your plants healthy?

"Plants are the source of the air we breathe and most of the food we eat, yet we often do not pay enough attention to keeping them healthy. This can have devastating results. FAO estimates that up to 40 percent of food crops are lost to plant pests and diseases annually. "

- Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations

Some benefits of growing healthy plants:

  • Resistant to pests and diseases.
  • Produces more food (fruits and vegetables).
  • Help purify the air and reduce pollution.
  • Can help improve mental health and reduce stress.

So whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, make sure you take good care of your plants to enjoy all the benefits they have to offer.
